Quick start

  1. Tap + on the main screen to add your first habit.
  2. Pick a name, color, and icon — that's it.
  3. Tap the circle on a habit card to mark it complete today.
  4. Tap the card to open the full grid with heat map + trend.

Lock Screen tap-complete

LiveHabit's hero feature lets you complete habits without unlocking.

  1. Settings → Pin to Lock Screen (toggle on) to start the Live Activity.
  2. Long-press your Lock Screen → Customize → add the LiveHabit widget.
  3. From any Lock Screen, tap each habit pill to complete.

Apple Watch

  1. Open Watch app on iPhone → My Watch tab.
  2. Scroll to LiveHabit → tap → enable "Show App on Apple Watch".
  3. Open LiveHabit on Watch → tap any habit to log on the spot.

iCloud sync

LiveHabit syncs across your iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch via your private iCloud account.

If sync doesn't work: Settings → [your name] → iCloud → confirm iCloud Drive is on, and that LiveHabit is enabled in Apps Using iCloud.

Import from HabitKit / Productive / Streaks

  1. In your source app: export your data as CSV.
  2. Send the CSV to your iPhone (AirDrop, Files, email).
  3. LiveHabit → Settings → Data → Import / Export → Import CSV.

Privacy

LiveHabit stores all habit data locally on your device + private iCloud. We never send your data to any server we control. No third-party analytics, no ads, no tracking.

Optional Motion & Fitness step counting is opt-in (Settings → Motion & Fitness). Steps are read on-device only via Core Motion — LiveHabit does not access Apple Health.
